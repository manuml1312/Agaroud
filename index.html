About Agaroud India

Preserving tradition, pioneering innovation

Heritage & Expertise

Agaroud India Agro Products LLP (AIAPL) stands as a premier agro products company rooted in the heart of Karnataka, Shivamogga. We are custodians of the ancient art of agarwood cultivation, combining centuries-old wisdom with modern innovation to deliver unparalleled quality.

Authentic Assam Legacy

Our expertise stems from authentic Assam traditions, where our specialists have been trained in the time-honored techniques passed down through generations. This Northeast Indian heritage forms the backbone of our 100% guaranteed inoculation success rate.

Global Standards, Local Roots

While we honor traditional methods, we've embraced international quality standards to serve premium markets across 25+ countries. Our state-of-the-art processing facilities ensure every product meets the stringent requirements of global luxury markets.

Sustainable Excellence

Our mission extends beyond commerce to conservation. We promote sustainable agarwood cultivation practices that protect this precious resource while meeting the growing international demand for authentic, premium-grade products.
